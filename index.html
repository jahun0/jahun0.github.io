About

Jahun Oh is a captain in the Republic of Korea Army and an assistant professor at the Korea Army Academy in Youngcheon. His work centers on applied AI and machine learning for real-world robotics and automation, with emphasis on continual learning, anomaly detection, and human-AI training systems. He has led projects on hyperautomation for defense administrative workflows, disinformation analytics, and VR-based SBIRT training with conversational agents. He holds an M.S. in Computer Engineering from Texas A&M University and a B.S. in Military Science and Information Science from the Korea Military Academy.

Research Interests

  • Continual learning for anomaly-based intrusion detection in operational networks
  • Cloud robotics offloading and deep reinforcement learning under network constraints
  • Conversational AI for training, simulation, and clinical skill development
  • Hyperautomation, process mining, and workflow orchestration for defense organizations
  • Human-AI decision support for disinformation and strategic analytics
